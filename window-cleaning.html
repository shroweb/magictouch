Window Cleaning

Streak-free, inside and out, using Unger German technology and a high-reach fed pole system — no ladders against your walls.

The method

Pure water, properly applied

Our deionised, 100% pure water system removes impurities from the water itself, so glass dries spotless with no detergent residue and no streaks. Paired with a high-reach fed pole system, we can safely clean windows up to four storeys high without scaffolding or ladders against the building.

Clean windows do more than look good — they let in more light and make a property feel better looked after, for staff, residents and visitors alike.
