Office Cleaning

Galway's specialist in high-grade office and commercial cleaning — built around a dedicated team that knows your building, your routines and your priorities.

Why it works

The same crew, every visit.

Most cleaning contracts rotate staff between buildings. Ours don't. Your office gets a dedicated team that learns the layout, the routines and what matters to your business — which means consistent quality, faster turnarounds, and far less risk from unfamiliar people holding a key to your premises.

Our cleaners are trained specifically for offices and commercial environments, where discretion and a consistently high standard matter as much as the clean itself. We'd rather build a long-term working relationship than chase one-off jobs — references from existing clients are available on request.

A weekly check-in with the owner or manager is a standard part of the service, not an add-on. We use it to review the work, talk through any issues, and adjust the plan as your needs change.
