Professional floor care you can trust — from routine maintenance to full restoration, across every floor type.

What we cover

Every surface, every condition

  • Strip and waxComplete finish removal and professional re-coating.
  • Deep cleaning & restorationBringing worn floors back, not just papering over the wear.
  • Scrub and recoatMid-cycle maintenance that extends the life of a finish.

Materials

Wood, vinyl, laminate, stone and marble

Different floors need different treatment, and the wrong product or pad can do real damage. We use safe, manufacturer-approved products with eco options available, applied by technicians who know which method suits which surface.

On-time scheduling, low-noise equipment and dust control mean the work fits around a working building, not the other way around. Contact us to schedule a free assessment and a custom care plan.
