Deep Clean

A true top-to-bottom clean, inside and out — professional-grade equipment, proper technique, and a checklist that doesn't skip the parts you can't reach.

Interior

Every surface, not just the visible ones

  • Ceiling to floorCeilings, cornices, vents, light fittings, switches and sockets.
  • High and low dustingCobweb removal, picture frames, shelves and décor.
  • Kitchen degreasingCooktops, splashbacks, extractor hoods and filters.
  • Oven deep cleanRacks, glass and door seals; hob and range detailing.
  • Bathroom detailTile and grout scrubbing; limescale, soap scum and mildew removal.
  • Toilet sanitisationInside, outside, hinges, base and behind.
  • FlooringHEPA-filtered vacuuming with edge and crevice detail.
  • Hard floorsMachine scrub, with scrub-and-recoat or strip-and-seal as needed.
  • CarpetsHot-water extraction (steam clean).
  • TouchpointsHandles, switches, remotes and thermostats sanitised.
  • Façade washSoffits, gutters (debris removal) and downpipes.
  • Outdoor surfacesPatios, decks and driveways — pressure washing and algae removal.
Who it's for

Move-in, move-out, or just overdue

A deep clean makes sense before a tenancy starts or ends, after a renovation, ahead of a big event, or simply when the everyday clean hasn't kept up with the year. We bring our own equipment and work to a fixed checklist, so nothing gets missed because it wasn't on anyone's mind that day.

Available for homes and businesses, as a one-off or as part of a regular maintenance plan alongside our other services.
