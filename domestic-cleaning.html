Domestic Cleaning

Regular or one-off house cleaning, fitted around your week — the same vetted cleaner each visit, not whoever's free.

How it works

Weekly, fortnightly, or whenever suits

Tell us the size of the house and how often you'd like us round — weekly, fortnightly, monthly, or a single deep tidy before guests arrive. We'll match you with a cleaner and keep them on your home for every visit, so you're not explaining where things go from scratch each time.

Bring your own preferred products if you have them, or we'll supply ours, including eco-friendly options on request.

What's covered

The full house, not just the visible rooms

  • KitchensSurfaces, appliance exteriors, hob, sink and floors.
  • BathroomsSanitising, descaling, and a proper scrub of tile and grout.
  • Bedrooms & living areasDusting, vacuuming, mopping and bed-making on request.
  • Laundry & ironingAvailable as an add-on to a regular clean.
  • Spring cleansA heavier one-off clean before or after a deep clean visit.
  • Move-in / move-outA full reset between tenancies or before a sale.
