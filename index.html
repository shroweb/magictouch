About us

A local crew, not a cleaning franchise.

Magic Touch was founded by Miroslav in 2019 with one mission: deliver cleaning that actually matches what's promised — and back it up every single time.

After years working in commercial cleaning across Ireland, Miroslav saw the same problem everywhere: strong first impressions, then falling standards once the contract was signed. So he built something different — a small, hand-picked team who take real pride in the work. People who turn up on time, communicate clearly, and leave a place genuinely clean, not just surface-tidied.

Seven years and 1,000+ jobs later, Magic Touch is still the same local business. Miroslav still takes every call himself, and the same crew that cleaned your building last month will be there next month.
