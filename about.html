Our story

One-stop cleaning, run properly

Magic Touch Galway started in 2019 as a one-stop solution for domestic and commercial cleaning across the city — somewhere a homeowner or a facilities manager could call once and have it handled, instead of juggling separate contractors for windows, floors and everything else.

Since then we've worked with more than 1,000 clients, added 16 new commercial accounts in 2025 alone, and kept the same approach: dedicated crews, clear communication, and showing up when we say we will. Most of our new work still comes from referrals — word of mouth from people who've already had us in.
